Evolution of India’s Credit Post IBC: Consumerization and why we should bring securitised loans to India

The paper talks about a fundamental shift in India’s credit landscape—credit flows moving away from industries and towards consumers. It also highlights the risk such a change poses, and suggests some policies that could reverse the trend.

Following this, we tried to model the said risk-aversion amongst banks using private bank level data, and analysed the impact on productivity.
